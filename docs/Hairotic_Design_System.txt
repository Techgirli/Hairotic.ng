
HAIROTIC.NG
DESIGN SYSTEM
Version 1.0  •  Prepared by Miracle  •  Status: Draft
Shop it like a premium fashion brand — not a traditional online store.
1. Design Philosophy
Hairotic.ng is not just another hair website. Every screen should communicate:
Confidence
Luxury
Simplicity
Boldness
Trust
The interface should make users feel as though they are shopping from a premium fashion brand rather than a traditional online store.
Design Principles

--- Table Start ---
Principle | What it means
Bold | Every page makes a statement. Never look generic.
Premium | Lots of whitespace. High-quality photography. Minimal clutter.
Human | Warm language. Helpful guidance. Friendly interactions.
Fast | Users should reach checkout within a few clicks.
Trustworthy | Transparent pricing. Real reviews. Clear policies. Professional design.
--- Table End ---

2. Brand Identity
Mission
To empower every woman to express her boldest, most confident self through premium hair that turns heads, inspires confidence, and never holds her back.
Vision
To become Nigeria's most loved premium hair brand, known for bold style, uncompromising quality, and unforgettable customer experiences — starting in Lagos and inspiring confidence nationwide.
Brand Personality
Bold  •  Stylish  •  Confident  •  Youthful  •  Creative  •  Honest  •  Premium
Voice
Confident  •  Playful  •  Friendly  •  Authentic  •  Supportive
Never sound
Corporate  •  Cold  •  Pushy  •  Overly salesy

4. Color System
Primary — Deep Orange (#E56717)
Used for: primary buttons, links, highlights, icons, active navigation, progress bars.
Secondary — White (#FFFFFF)
Used for: backgrounds, cards, forms, sections.
Dark — Charcoal (#222222)
Used for: text, icons, navigation.
Gray (#6B7280)
Used for: secondary text, labels, descriptions.
Light Gray (#F8F8F8)
Used for: cards, section backgrounds.
Semantic Colors

--- Table Start ---
